🚀 Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with Voice Scribe in under 2 minutes.

Install the Extension
  1. Open Chrome Web Store
  2. Search for "Voice Transcription + AI"
  3. Click "Add to Chrome"
  4. Wait for installation to complete
  5. The Voice Scribe icon 🎙️ appears in your Chrome toolbar
Open Voice Scribe
  1. Click the Voice Scribe icon in your toolbar
  2. The Side Panel opens on the right side of Chrome
  3. First-time users will see a 3-step onboarding tour (you can skip it)
Grant Microphone Permission
  1. Click the Settings gear (⚙️) in the top-right
  2. Select your preferred language from the dropdown
  3. Click the microphone button 🎙️
  4. When Chrome asks, click "Allow" to grant microphone access
Start Transcribing
  1. Click the large microphone button in the center
  2. Start speaking clearly into your microphone
  3. Watch your words appear in real-time
  4. Click the button again to stop recording
Export Your Text
  1. Click "Export PDF" to open a print-ready version
  2. Or click the dropdown and select "Guardar .TXT" to download
  3. Use "Copiar" to copy everything to clipboard instantly
You're ready! Voice Scribe is now set up and ready to use.

🔧 Troubleshooting & Solutions

Common problems and how to fix them.

Symptoms:

  • Microphone button doesn't work
  • No permission prompt appears
  • Error message about permissions

Solutions:

Method 1: Chrome Settings

  1. Click the 🔒 lock icon in Chrome's address bar
  2. Find "Microphone" in the list
  3. Toggle to "Allow"
  4. Reload Voice Scribe (close and reopen side panel)

Method 2: Chrome Content Settings

  1. Go to chrome://settings/content/microphone
  2. Scroll to "Not allowed to use your microphone"
  3. Find Voice Scribe in the list
  4. Click the trash icon to remove the block
  5. Reload Voice Scribe

Method 3: System Permissions (macOS)

  1. Open System Preferences → Security & Privacy
  2. Go to Privacy tab
  3. Select Microphone
  4. Ensure Chrome is checked
  5. Restart Chrome

Symptoms:

  • Microphone button shows recording
  • Waveform animates
  • But no text appears

Possible Causes & Solutions:

Cause 1: Wrong Input Device Selected

  1. Click system volume icon (taskbar/menu bar)
  2. Open sound settings
  3. Ensure correct microphone is selected as input
  4. Test mic in system settings

Cause 2: Microphone Muted

  1. Check if mic has physical mute switch
  2. Check system volume mixer
  3. Ensure mic isn't muted in Chrome

Cause 3: Web Speech API Issue

  1. Close and reopen side panel
  2. Refresh Chrome completely
  3. Check internet connection
  4. Try a different language temporarily

Symptoms:

  • Many wrong words
  • Gibberish text
  • Missing words

Solutions:

Improve Microphone Quality:

  • Use a headset microphone (best option)
  • Position mic 2-4 inches from mouth
  • Avoid laptop built-in mic if possible
  • Test mic quality in another app

Reduce Background Noise:

  • Close windows (street noise)
  • Turn off fans/AC
  • Move to quieter room
  • Use noise-cancelling microphone

Speak Clearly:

  • Normal pace (not too fast)
  • Enunciate words clearly
  • Don't mumble
  • Pause between sentences

🔒 Privacy & Permissions

Your privacy is our top priority.

What Permissions Does Voice Scribe Need?

Voice Scribe requests only 2 permissions:

Permission Why It's Needed What It Does
storage Save your data locally Stores transcripts, settings, preferences in chrome.storage.local
sidePanel Display the interface Opens Voice Scribe in Chrome's Side Panel instead of popup

What Voice Scribe Does NOT Do

❌ Never Does Explanation
Collect audio Your voice is processed by Web Speech API, not sent to Voice Scribe servers
Transmit transcripts Your text stays 100% on your device
Track browsing No analytics, no tracking, no cookies
Access other tabs Cannot see or access your other browser tabs
Read personal data No access to emails, passwords, or personal info
Show ads No advertisements, ever
Your Data Stays Private: Voice Scribe has no backend server. All data stays in chrome.storage.local. Only you can access your transcripts. Uninstalling deletes all data automatically.

When reporting a bug, please include:

  • Steps to reproduce: What you did before the bug appeared

💡 Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Speak clearly and at a normal pace for best transcription accuracy.
  • A dedicated headset greatly improves recognition compared to a built-in mic.
  • A stable internet connection ensures consistent speech recognition performance.
  • Minimize background noise by closing windows and turning off fans/AC when possible.
  • Export important transcripts regularly to avoid losing them if you clear browser data.
