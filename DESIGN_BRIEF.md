## 1. Visual Strategy
To match the "Globalist" and "Premium" vision, we selected a **Cyberpunk/SaaS aesthetic** similar to Linear, Vercel, or Raycast.
- **Color Mode:** Dark Mode (Professional, Developer-focused).
- **Primary Accent:** Deep Purple/Blue Gradients (`#5b13ec`) to match VoiceScribe's identity and convey "Tech/AI".
- **Typography:** `Inter` (Clean, legible, modern).
- **Effects:** Glassmorphism on cards to add depth without clutter.

## 2. Generated Prompt
This prompt was sent to the AI Designer to ensure all context from `HOMEPAGE_COPY.md` was included:

> "Create a premium, high-converting landing page for 'BitNomade'.
> Visual Style: Dark mode, deep purple/blue gradients (cyberpunk/saas aesthetic), clean sans-serif typography (Inter), glassmorphism effects on cards.
> Content:
> 1. Hero Section:
>    - H1: "Recupera el 22% de tu presupuesto de marketing desperdiciado por un tracking ciego."
>    - Sub: "BitNomade detiene la hemorragia de capital en tu infraestructura digital..."
>    - Primary CTA: "Detener Fugas de Ingresos" (Gradient button).
> 2. Metrics Bar: "+10,000 Profesionales usan nuestras herramientas."
> 3. Value Pillars (3-Column Grid):
>    - "Auditoría Global", "UX Predictivo", "Código Auditable".
> 4. The Showcase (Big 2x2 Grid of Feature Cards):
>    - Ultimate PDF, VoiceScribe AI, Snatch WhatsApp, WhatsAppBoost.
> 5. Footer: "Blindemos tu infraestructura." Buttons: "Solicitar Auditoría", "GitHub"."

## 3. Design Components
- **Hero:** Impactful, centered, with a glowing "nebula" background/gradient to catch attention immediately.
- **Showcase Grid:** Large cards for the "Big 4" extensions. Each card features:
    - High-contrast border.
    - Neon accent.
    - Clear ROI headline (e.g., "Ahorra $200/año").
- **Trust Indicators:** Technical icons (Shield, Layers, Sparkles) to communicate "Engineering Precision".
